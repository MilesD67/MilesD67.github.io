Dear Data Postcard

In the dear data postcard I create data on what I am curious about how much time I spend in my free time. The question I have is “When I’m not at school what do I do in my free time/when I don't have anything important?” The data is gathered on a pie chart that is on different time periods of the day. One is “school” another is things I do after school such as “basketball” and last is “freetime”. I will need to gather 7 days in the week after school. I will use a piece of paper at that time then use google sheets at the end of the day.
